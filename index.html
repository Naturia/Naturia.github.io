FaceHeart-rPPG

An efficient non-contact application for heart rate measurement

New

Remote photoplethysmography (rPPG) provides a convenient and non-contact heart rate measurement, which can be used in natural light conditions to record human face video images using a common camera. However, this kind of experiment is currently sensitive to light, movement and skin color.

In this research, we introduce a simple and robust heart rate signal extraction method. It can achieve high accuracy, with much-reduced error rate. The design has the characteristics of fast and convenient operation, with low cost and non-contact feature, which is suitable for wider monitoring heart rate in daily life.

PlantPano - Plant Species IdentificationOpen Source

People often see unknown plants.Indentifying them is of great significance. In this research, We apply deep convolutional neural network to automatic plant recognition and develop a mobile app for identifying plant species.

The recognition process is trained by TensorFlow inception v3 Net and the application is implemented with various modules such as User registration, Picturing and Image uploading, Classification with pre-trained model, as well as Process of parsing and displaying the result. The application shows a high accuracy and good user experience.

Wavelet Noise Removal

Polar ice sheets play an import role in the global climate system. Radio-echo sounding (RES) can be used to understand ice-sheet processes through measurement of ice thickness, englacial flow structures and bed properties, making it one of the most popular tools in glaciological exploration. In this research, we use wavelet and 2D FFT filtering to remove strip noise for airborne radar data. Model tests and field-data processing demonstrate the method removes horizontal and vertical strip noise well, and the strong first reflector from the ice surface, improving RES data.
